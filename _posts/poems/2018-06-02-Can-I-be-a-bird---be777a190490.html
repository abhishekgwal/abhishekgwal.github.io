Can I be a bird ?

Can I be a bird With the wings to fly Not of feather But the desire to sway high

Can I be a bird 
With the wings to fly
Not of feather
But the desire to sway high

Can I be a bird
Small , but vision so vivid and round
Can I be swift and yet so, agile

Can I be a bird 
Of facing the wind
Of pushing high 
Of Crashing, but the strength of miles.

Can I be a bird 
Of flock
Of team
Of effort
Of mellow and calm

Can I be a bird , of this life

By Divya Godayal on June 2, 2018.
