Yes, I do

I love you, she said. Yes, I do.

I love you, she said. 
Yes, I do.

I do, for you didn’t let me go.
I do, for I told you no.
I do, for every moment was ours.
I do, for you talked to me for hours.

I do, for you just looked at me.
I do, for I was the only one you could see.
I do, for we gazed at the stars.
I do, for we forgot all wars.

I do, for I was in you.
I do, for you still feel so fresh as morning dew.
I do, for we are one.
I do, for you are never done.

I do, for you are in my mind.
I do, for you are my forward and my rewind.
I do, for you still give me the chills.
I do, hope you will.

By Divya Godayal on June 11, 2018.
