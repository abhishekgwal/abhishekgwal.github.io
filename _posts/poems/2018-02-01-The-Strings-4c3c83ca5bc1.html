The Strings

Melodious as it may Sweet as it could get. Day by day. Night by night. Every moment of a new chord. Every new prayer to the old lord.

Melodious as it may
Sweet as it could get.
Day by day.
Night by night.
Every moment of a new chord.
Every new prayer to the old lord.

My strings of happiness 
are played by you now.
A few highs and some lows.
Writing a new song
of old beginning and a new End!!

By Divya Godayal on February 1, 2018.
