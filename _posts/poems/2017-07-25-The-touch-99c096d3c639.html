The touch

Last time I was all in with all my emotions within My hand held tight and I don’t know if it was right.

Last time I was all in 
with all my emotions within 
My hand held tight 
and I don’t know if it was right.

And yes it was love 
that deep deep love 
The depth the darkness
The shudder the kiss 
The thunder of the nights 
The emotions which gained new heights

And it was nothing but love
Not physical a touch
When we didn’t even hug 
But I could still feel so much

May be you didn’t see
May be I didn’t show 
But I
Never longed for more

But our story was different 
Yours 
And mine 
I headed left 
And you wanted right.

And when I still think what it is 
while making something new

I am holding it
but still letting it go 
My eyes are all open 
but this dream is true

My heart is a mess
My brain has quest 
I hear those new foot steps 
Still hoping if I can get the best

And you know what
I have no image of you 
No expectations
No shame 
Just a silhouette of emotions 
To meet someone with no game

When you don’t see me 
And I don't see you 
When our heart just feels 
Every beat 
When I have someone to count on
till the very end.

By Divya Godayal on July 25, 2017.
