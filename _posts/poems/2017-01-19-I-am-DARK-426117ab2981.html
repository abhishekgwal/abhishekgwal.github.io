I am DARK

You touch me I tremble Not with excitement, but with fear. I am DARK within, Oh! don’t come near.

You touch me 
I tremble
Not with excitement, but with fear.
I am DARK within,
Oh! don’t come near.

I am a broken
I am wounded
But you say you can fix me. 
I might heal, it’s not a big deal,
You whisper in my ear.

My heart rushes 
Emotion gushes
Mind overworked and stopped.
Please do not come near.
If you fear the DARK!

By Divya Godayal on January 19, 2017.
