Do you feel ?

When your mother’s eyes Are wet Of the pain She dealt Do you feel ?

When your mother’s eyes 
Are wet 
Of the pain 
She dealt 
Do you feel ?

When your partner hugs you tight
And feels just right 
Overwhelmed!
Do you feel?

When your friend held your hand.
Tight and firm.
And said, don’t go.
Cause he just feels too low.
Oh! Do you feel?

When your dog kisses your face 
Walks side by side 
Sits next and its heart at pace 
Do you feel ?

And then you were just away from home
A poor lad 
Looked grim and sad
Now do you feel ?

Everyone has a reason to feel. I know you feel it too. I hope you do, cause life is all about feelings.

Just close your eyes and feel all that comes your way. FEEL IT !

By Divya Godayal on October 20, 2017.
